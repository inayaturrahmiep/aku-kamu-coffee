About Us

Aku Kamu Coffee is a cozy coffee shop located in the vibrant city of Bandung, Indonesia. Our mission is to create a warm and inviting space where coffee enthusiasts can come together to enjoy exceptional coffee and build meaningful connections.

At Aku Kamu Coffee, we believe in the power of coffee to bring people closer. Our team of skilled baristas is dedicated to crafting each cup with passion and precision. We source the finest beans from local coffee farms and meticulously roast them to perfection, ensuring a rich and flavorful coffee experience.

Location: Aku Kamu Coffee
Jalan Cikapundung No. 123 Bandung, Indonesia
Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
